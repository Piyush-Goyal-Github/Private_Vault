About Us

E-Store.com is an technology company based in Seattle that focuses on e-commerce. It is considered one of the Big Four technology companies, along with Google, Apple, and Microsoft. It has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world" as well as the world's most valuable brand. E-Store is known for its disruption of well-established industries through technological innovation and mass scale. E-Store is the largest Internet company by revenue in the world. It is the second largest private employer in the United States and one of the world's most valuable companies.

E-Store was founded on July 5, 2010. The company started as an online marketplace for books but expanded to sell electronics, software, video games, apparel, furniture, food, toys, and jewelry. In 2015, E-Store surpassed Walmart as the most valuable retailer in the United States by market capitalization. In 2017, E-Store acquired Whole Foods Market for 1.4 billion, substantially increasing E-Store's footprint as a physical retailer. In 2018, Bezos announced that its two-day delivery service, E-Store Prime, had surpassed 100 million subscribers worldwide. E-Store distributes downloads and streaming of video, music, and audiobooks through its Prime Video, E-Store Music, Twitch, and Audible subsidiaries. E-Store also has a publishing arm, E-Store Publishing, a film and television studio, E-Store Studios, and a cloud computing subsidiary, E-Store Web Services. It produces consumer electronics including Kindle e-readers, Fire tablets, Fire TV, and Echo devices.
